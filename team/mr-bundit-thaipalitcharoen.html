Mr. Bundit Thaipalitcharoen

Senior Litigator & Specialist in Criminal Procedure Law
Schedule a Consultation

With over 20 years of experience in the Thai judicial system, Mr. Bundit Thaipalitcharoen is a distinguished litigator specializing in Criminal Defense and Strategic Case Analysis.

A member of the Thai Bar (Batch 62) and a Master of Laws (LL.M.) from Thammasat University, he is renowned for his Case Autopsy: Masterful Litigation Tactics for Professional Lawyers (Pha Samnuan)—a methodical approach to legal defense used to train professional lawyers.

In addition to his litigation practice, he is a certified researcher through the National Research Development Institute of Thailand, blending his extensive courtroom experience with deep academic and legislative insights.

Areas of Expertise

  • Criminal Litigation & Procedure Law
  • Pre-Litigation Strategy & Investigation Analysis
  • Representation in Court of First Instance, Appeal & Supreme Court
  • Environmental Law
  • Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR) Legislative Frameworks

Professional Experience

  • Developed and lectured on the "Case Autopsy" (Pha Samnuan) method, co-lecturing on criminal litigation techniques with former Presiding Justice Chonabot Suphasiri to train the next generation of professional lawyers.
  • Served as a high-level committee member for the Lawyers Council of Thailand, contributing to dispute mediation, professional development curricula, and the subcommittee on Human Rights and Children’s Rights.
  • Published authoritative legal texts and research, including books on winning techniques for lawyers and a case study on "Dismissed Cases" involving false reports and perjury.
  • Served as a Co-researcher for the Department of Industrial Works, focusing on amending factory laws to incentivize Corporate Social Responsibility (CSR).
