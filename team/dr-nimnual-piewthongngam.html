Dr. Nimnual Piewthongngam

Founder & Chief Strategist
Schedule a Consultation

Dr. Nimnual Piewthongngam is the Founder and Chief Strategist of Expatique, a premium advisory and client-support platform dedicated to assisting international and Thai clients in navigating business, legal, investment, and lifestyle matters in Thailand and across borders.

With more than two decades of professional experience, she has built a reputation as a trusted advisor known for combining strategic insight, legal coordination, international business understanding, and extensive cross-sector networks to help clients solve complex challenges with discretion and precision.

Throughout her career, Dr. Nimnual has worked closely with foreign investors, entrepreneurs, executives, institutions, and private clients on matters involving market entry, business expansion, regulatory coordination, strategic partnerships, risk management, government relations, and cross-border operations. Her strength lies not only in understanding the legal and advisory landscape, but also in knowing how to navigate systems, connect the right people, and move projects forward effectively in real-world environments.

In addition to her advisory and executive work, Dr. Nimnual served as a university professor for more than 20 years, giving her a rare combination of academic depth, analytical thinking, and practical business expertise. Her background spans sustainability strategy, international business development, stakeholder engagement, and high-level project coordination across Thailand and overseas markets.

Over the years, she has developed an extensive network across the legal, corporate, governmental, diplomatic, and international business communities, enabling Expatique to provide clients with trusted access to professional support and reliable strategic guidance. Her approach is highly personalized, solution-oriented, and built on long-term relationships, professionalism, and confidence.

Today, through Expatique, Dr. Nimnual continues to support clients ranging from private individuals to international businesses seeking trusted guidance, strong local connections, and sophisticated advisory support in Thailand and beyond.
