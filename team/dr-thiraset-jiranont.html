Dr. Thiraset Jiranont

Principal Tax Strategist
Schedule a Consultation

With over 15 years of experience in accounting, taxation, forensic accounting, and academic leadership, Dr. Thiraset Metheeranont is widely recognized as one of Thailand’s leading experts in strategic accounting and financial management.

Holding a Doctor of Management Science in Accounting from Xiamen University, China, he has built a distinguished career spanning higher education, corporate advisory, taxation consultancy, and executive financial strategy. His expertise combines rigorous academic research with practical industry application, making him a respected authority among entrepreneurs, corporations, and public sector institutions.

Dr. Thiraset specializes in Strategic Financial Management, Tax Planning, Forensic Accounting, Managerial Accounting, Budgeting for Performance Control, and Accounting Standards for both Publicly Accountable Entities (PAEs) and Non-Publicly Accountable Entities (NPAEs). He is also highly regarded for his work in CFO strategy development, SME financial advisory, and bridging the gap between accounting systems and taxation compliance.

In addition to his professional practice, Dr. Thiraset has played a major role in developing Thailand’s accounting and entrepreneurial ecosystem through academic instruction, government training programs, and consultancy projects. He has served as a lecturer and advisor for numerous business development initiatives, including programs under the Ministry of Commerce, the Revenue Department, and the Department of Industrial Promotion.

Experience

  • Served as Director of C Ribbon Accounting and Tax Co., Ltd. and C Accounting and Enterprise Co., Ltd., providing strategic accounting, taxation, and financial advisory services for businesses and entrepreneurs.
  • Worked as a full-time Accounting Lecturer at Ubon Ratchathani University for over 15 years, contributing extensively to academic development, research, and professional training.
  • Appointed as an Independent Director of Ubon Ratchathani Rajabhat University and served on the Committee for Research and Academic Services.
  • Delivered lectures and professional training programs on accounting standards, taxation systems, SME financial management, forensic accounting, and strategic CFO planning for entrepreneurs, government agencies, and private institutions across Thailand.
  • Served as a counselor and advisor for entrepreneurship and knowledge-based organization (KBO) development projects under the Department of Industrial Promotion.
