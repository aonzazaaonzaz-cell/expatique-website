Ms. Jinnarat Waraboonnakphirom is a certified insurance professional and qualified lawyer, holding a formal life insurance agent license with Krungthai-AXA Life Insurance.

With over 20 years of expertise, she integrates her deep knowledge of insurance and protection strategies with a strong background in human resources and welfare development.

Jinnarat holds a Master of Labour and Welfare Development from Thammasat University, a Bachelor of Laws (LL.B.) from Sukhothai Thammathirat University, and a Bachelor of Arts in French from Chiang Mai University.
