Who we are

Your Trusted Partner for Expats Living in Thailand

Expatique is dedicated to supporting expatriates as they navigate the legal, administrative, and practical aspects of living in Thailand. We serve as a trusted point of connection between international clients and a network of qualified professionals across key sectors, helping individuals and families access reliable expertise with confidence.

Through our established network, we facilitate access to services in areas such as legal and estate matters, visa and immigration processes, medical coordination, insurance solutions, and education opportunities. Our approach is centred on professionalism, discretion, and efficiency, ensuring that clients are connected with experienced practitioners who understand both the Thai regulatory environment and the needs of the expatriate community.

Our work is guided by our Founder, Dr. Nimnual Piewthongngam, who brings more than 20 years of experience in international law, business consulting, and ASEAN economic and regulatory affairs. Her extensive background in legal advisory, academia, and international business development provides strategic insight and leadership to our mission to assist expatriates and international clients in navigating complex systems in Thailand.

At Expatique, we believe that the right expertise and trusted professional relationships are essential to managing life abroad effectively. Whether clients are establishing themselves in Thailand, addressing legal or personal matters, or planning for the future, our role is to connect them with the professionals and support they need to move forward with clarity and confidence.
