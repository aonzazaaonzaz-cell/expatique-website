Overview

Clear Pathways for Your Stay

At Expatique, we provide personalised consultation and guidance for individuals, families, students, professionals, and long-term residents seeking to build their lives in Thailand with clarity and confidence.

We assist with educational visas, long-term stay options, work permit pathways, residency applications, and family-based support visas, helping clients understand the requirements, prepare documentation, and navigate each process with ease.

  • The Education (ED) Visa is a common option for individuals who wish to stay in Thailand while participating in structured learning programs. This includes educational activities such as Language studies or cultural courses, where students enrol in certified programs that meet immigration requirements.
  • The Destination Thailand (DTV) Visa is designed for individuals who wish to spend an extended period in Thailand while remaining professionally active or participating in cultural activities.
  • Non-Immigrant Visa (For Work Permit) is designed for professionals, entrepreneurs, and employees seeking legal employment in Thailand. The process is an essential step toward working and conducting business lawfully.
  • Permanent Residency and Citizenship Visa is an option for individuals seeking to establish long-term roots in Thailand. These pathways may offer greater stability, security, and long-term benefits without annual visa renewals.
  • Family Support Visa options are intended to help spouses, children, parents, and dependents remain together legally while residing in Thailand.

ED VISA
(EDUCATION)

  • 90-Day Stay (Extendable)
  • For structured, full-time training
  • Requires application abroad

We provide professional legal coordination for students and individuals pursuing specialized training in Thailand. We manage the complex administrative requirements to ensure your stay is fully compliant with Thai immigration laws.

  • Eligibility & Application Strategy: Expert guidance on the initial 90-day extendable visa, including application coordination from outside Thailand.
  • Structured Training Coordination: Support for applicants requiring structured training schedules and certified documentation from recognised institutions.
  • SAT Verification: Facilitating mandatory Sports Authority of Thailand (SAT) verification for specialised sports and self-defence training visas.
  • Certified Documentation: Securing and organising all necessary training letters, school certifications, and government-stamped documents.
  • Extension Management: Proactive management of the periodic extension process to ensure an uninterrupted stay for the duration of your studies.

DTV VISA
(DESTINATION THAILAND)

  • 5-Year Validity (Multiple Entry)
  • 180 Days Stay per Entry
  • 180-Day Extension Available
  • Up to 1-Year Continuous Stay
  • Remote Work Allowed for Foreign Employers
  • 500,000 THB Financial Requirement
  • Family Inclusion (Spouse & Children Under 20)
  • Visa Fee ~10,000 THB (≈400 USD), but may vary by Embassy.
  • 90-Day Reporting Required
  • Application via Thai e-Visa (Outside Thailand)

Category-specific documents:

Workation (Remote work, freelancers, or business owners):

  • Contract or client agreements
  • Certificate of employment or business registration
  • Payslips
  • Recent tax filings
  • Professional portfolio

Thai Soft Power (Muay Thai, cooking classes, or medical treatments):

  • Letter of acceptance from a recognized organization
  • Business registration document of the institution
  • Program duration must be at least six months

Dependents (spouse or child under 20):

  • Marriage certificate for spouses
  • Birth certificate for children

DTV Visa Eligibility Criteria

  • Minimum Age: 20 years
  • Immigration Record: Clean history with Thai Immigration
  • Criminal Record: No serious criminal convictions
  • Financial Requirement: 500,000 THB in a bank account
  • Funds Rule: Must show funds during application and extensions
  • Not Accepted: Crypto or investment portfolios
  • Application Location: Must apply from outside Thailand
