Overview

Planning for the Future

Planning for the future is an important step for anyone living abroad.

At Expatique, we understand that matters related to wills, estates, and family arrangements require both professionalism and care. We work with experienced legal professionals who can assist expatriates in organising their affairs and ensuring their wishes are clearly documented and legally protected.

In sensitive situations, such as the passing of a loved one, having the right support and coordination can make an incredibly difficult time more manageable.
Our network of legal professionals provides guidance and assistance to help families navigate these situations with compassion and clarity.

Services include:

  • Will drafting and estate planning
  • Probate and estate management
  • Foreign death and repatriation services, including:
    1. Hospital and medical coordination
    2. Embassy and consular coordination
    3. Funeral and mortuary arrangements
    4. Repatriation of human remains
    5. Assistance and coordination for families and relatives

These services are designed to provide expatriates and their families with reassurance, clarity, and professional support during important and often sensitive life situations.
