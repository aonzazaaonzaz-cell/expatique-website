What is the Thailand BOI?

The Thailand Board of Investment (BOI) is a government agency that promotes investment in industries contributing to Thailand's economic growth and innovation. BOI promotion offers incentives and strategic advantages for eligible foreign businesses operating in Thailand.

Supporting Foreign Investment in Thailand

Main Objectives of the BOI

Encourage both local and foreign investment

Promote technology and innovation

Support sustainable economic development

Enhance Thailand's global competitiveness

Develop strategic industries and supply chains
