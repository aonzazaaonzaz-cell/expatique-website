Overview

Navigating family and domestic legal matters requires not only legal precision but also discretion, strategic guidance, and a deep understanding of both local and international legal frameworks.

At Expatique, we provide tailored legal consultation and representation for individuals, couples, expatriates, and international families seeking trusted support in Thailand. Our team is committed to safeguarding your personal interests, financial security, family relationships, and long-term future through professional, confidential, and client-focused legal solutions.

Our Expertise

  • Matrimonial & Marital Agreements
    Professional drafting and review of Prenuptial and Postnuptial Agreements designed to protect personal and marital assets, alongside legal assistance for separation and divorce proceedings in Thailand.
  • Child Custody & Family Protection
    Strategic legal support in matters involving child custody disputes, parental rights, legal legitimation of children, and international child abduction cases, with careful consideration for the welfare and protection of all parties involved.
  • Asset Division & Spousal Support
    Experienced negotiation and litigation services relating to marital asset division, financial settlements, alimony, and spousal support to ensure fair and legally compliant outcomes.
  • Wills, Estates & Succession Planning
    Comprehensive succession planning services, including Will drafting, Estate administration, inheritance matters, and the appointment of Estate Executors to help secure and preserve your legacy for future generations.
  • Cross-Border Family Legal Consultation
    Guidance for expatriates and international families navigating complex legal matters involving multiple jurisdictions, foreign assets, international marriages, and residency-related family concerns.

At Expatique, we understand that family-related legal matters require sensitivity, confidentiality, and trusted legal counsel. Our goal is to provide seamless guidance and practical legal solutions while helping clients navigate each stage with clarity and confidence.
