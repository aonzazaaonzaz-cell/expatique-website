What is the Eastern Economic Corridor?

The Eastern Economic Corridor (EEC) is Thailand's flagship economic development program, established under the EEC Act of 2018. It covers three eastern provinces — Chachoengsao, Chonburi, and Rayong — and aims to transform the region into a leading ASEAN economic zone.

The EEC offers a comprehensive package of incentives to attract both domestic and foreign investment in 12 targeted industries. These include tax holidays, streamlined permits, special visa programs, and world-class infrastructure including high-speed rail, airports, and deep-sea ports.

Managed by the EEC Office under the Prime Minister's Office, the corridor builds on Thailand's existing industrial strengths while driving innovation in next-generation industries such as robotics, digital technology, and biotechnology.

EEC Benefits & Incentives

Corporate Income Tax Exemption

Up to 13 years of corporate income tax exemption for targeted industries, with additional 50% reduction for up to 5 more years.

Import Duty Exemption

Exemption on import duties for machinery, raw materials, and materials used for R&D purposes within EEC zones.

Personal Income Tax Reduction

Flat 17% personal income tax rate for qualified specialists and executives working in EEC-promoted activities.

Investment Grants

Competitive enhancement grants and R&D funding support for qualifying projects within EEC priority industries.

4-Year Smart Visa

Up to 4-year visa for highly skilled professionals, investors, executives, and startup entrepreneurs in targeted industries.

No Work Permit Required

Smart Visa holders are exempt from the work permit requirement, reducing bureaucratic burden and processing time.

Family Inclusion

Dependents (spouse and children) receive the same visa duration and work authorization benefits under the Smart Visa program.

100% Foreign Ownership

Foreign nationals may own 100% of a company in EEC-promoted activities, bypassing the Foreign Business Act restrictions.

Land Ownership Rights

Foreign investors may own land within EEC zones for promoted activities, with leases up to 50 years (renewable for another 49 years).

One-Stop Service Center

Streamlined permits and licenses through a single service center, with expedited processing for visas, work permits, and business registrations.
