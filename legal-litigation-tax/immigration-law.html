Overview

Relocating to Thailand or managing long-term residency requirements can involve complex legal procedures, evolving immigration regulations, and extensive documentation.

At Expatique, we provide strategic immigration and residency solutions tailored to expatriates, investors, retirees, business professionals, and families seeking a seamless transition into life and business in Thailand. Our team offers professional guidance, personalised consultation, and end-to-end support to help clients navigate every stage of the immigration process with confidence, efficiency, and full legal compliance.

Our Expertise

  • Visa & Residency Solutions
    Specialized assistance with Long-Term Resident (LTR) Visas, Marriage Visas, Retirement Visas, Family Support Visas, Destination Thailand Visas (DTV), Education Visas, and Thailand Elite Membership programs for individuals seeking long-term residency options in Thailand.
  • Corporate Immigration & Work Permits
    Comprehensive support for Work Permit applications, Non-Immigrant Business Visas, BOI-related immigration matters, and corporate compliance services for expatriate professionals, executives, and international businesses operating in Thailand.
  • Permanent Residency Applications
    Professional guidance through Thailand’s highly selective Permanent Residency application process, including eligibility assessments, documentation preparation, interview support, and submission management.
  • Immigration Compliance & Legal Representation
    Representation before the Thai Immigration Bureau for complex immigration matters, visa status issues, overstays, residency concerns, appeals, and regulatory compliance to help ensure a lawful and uninterrupted stay in Thailand.
  • Tailored Consultation for Expats & International Families
    Personalised immigration strategies designed to support expatriates, remote workers, retirees, investors, and international families based on their long-term residency, lifestyle, and business objectives.

At Expatique, we understand that immigration matters require precision, reliability, and up-to-date legal knowledge. Our mission is to simplify the process, minimise uncertainty, and provide clients with trusted legal support for building their future in Thailand.
